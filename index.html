Abstract

This report presents comprehensive data quality verification results for the RP2 clinical dataset, comprising 11,026 patient records across 17 HIV-related studies conducted in Johannesburg, South Africa. Following rigorous quality assurance procedures, all numeric missing codes have been removed, extreme values corrected, and measurement units standardized. Ten studies (9,987 participants, 90.6% of dataset) have been verified as production-ready for analysis. The remaining studies require data source re-extraction due to systematic extraction failures. This documentation provides detailed study profiles, quality correction summaries, and guidance for appropriate dataset utilization in clinical research and epidemiological analysis.
